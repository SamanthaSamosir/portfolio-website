In 2018, I chose to study IT, driven by my passion for solving puzzles and understanding technology. My curiosity about diverse tech environments led me to explore various industries, such as pharmaceuticals, telemedicine, and business, and take on roles like full-stack developer, QA automation specialist, and DevOps engineer.

Volunteering with children at Yayasan Kasih Anak Kanker Indonesia (YKAKI) and orphanages, teaching programming or English, sparked my love for education. After graduating, I continued this passion by teaching kids programming, while also challenging myself as a QA Analyst.

Currently, I’m diving into cybersecurity, driven by concerns about Indonesia's digital safety. I'm part of the Digital Talent Scholarship by Kominfo and seeking scholarships for a master’s in cybersecurity. My goal is to become an expert, offer local training, and launch "Melek," a startup focused on research and solutions. I aspire to collaborate with KOMINFO and BRIN to enhance Indonesia’s cybersecurity.
